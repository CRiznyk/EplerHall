Introduction

Stephen Epler Hall Building is a building located on the Portland State University (PSU) Campus. It can be found on the corner of SW 12th Avenue and Montgomery Street. The building was constructed in 2003 and named after Stepehn Epler who was a founder at PSU. Epler was recruited to counsel veterans returning from World War II who wanted to pursue higher education. Epler was known for his advocacy for education and worked hard to establish PSU yet never had an administrative role. Instead this building honors his work as a founder.
